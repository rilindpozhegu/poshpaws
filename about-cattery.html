CATTERY

Cattery


Cattery

We have 40 units in the cattery, these building are A/C and each unit is equipped with play mats, table and toys. Each unit has its own private outside garden which is shaded, containing tree stumps as great viewing and scratch posts. They can sit and watch the goats and camels wander by and birds chatting on the fences. Cats are fed with Royal Canin and a variety of soft food including Waitrose, Friskies, Whiskas etc.

We have just recently opened our new Cattery in Al Khawaneej where we have 18 large luxurious catteries. All the Catteries are AC controlled inside and have fully enclosed outdoor runs so your cat can enjoy the view & fresh farm air. Each Cattery is equipt with scratch posts, scratch mats, toys and of course our cat loving staff who go the extra mile to ensure your cat is happy & well looked after. Posh Paws offer a Pet Collection & Delivery service to both Dubai & Sharjah, Professional Grooming, and free delivery of Pet Food & Pet Products.
