About

KENNELS

Kennels


Kennels

We have in total 102 kennels, 42 large and 60 medium, all are equipped with A/C and each have their own private shaded garden. In addition we have 12 communal areas, all with paddling pools, tunnels and toys. These areas are shaded and grassed and have great views of the farm with its acres of land. Dogs are fed twice daily with Royal Canin biscuits and Butch meat, special diets can also be catered for. All dogs are given Frontline on entry to the kennel at a charge of Dhs 60 to protect against ticks and fleas, ticks being a very common problem in the U.A.E.
