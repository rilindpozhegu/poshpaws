Petting Farm

Located at Al Hamra Stables, Dubai is a most amazing petting farm. The Petting farm is open 7 days a week, you can feed and pet the animals, have a pony ride or simply relax and enjoy our coffee shop overlooking the farm. Nearly all the animals at the petting farm are donated or rescued animals, some needing homes. We are supported by Poshpaws boarding kennels and cattery. Volunteers are always needed in the farm and for outside events and adoption days, contact us on 056 6044091. We have many pets for adoption or fostering from hamsters, birds, dogs and cats. Animals are available for hire and even photo shoots!!

Sponsorship certificates available.

School Trips to Farm / School Visits

An excellent way to give students of all ages a fun filled educational experience is a trip to our farm, they can pet, hold and feed their favorite animals. We can also bring the animals to your school, ideal for the younger age groups. Firsthand experience allows them to appreciate the animal’s needs and understand that animals need love too!
